Frequently asked

What's free, and what's in Pro?
Free covers the daily habit: logging your sessions and rolls, your Home screen, full history and search, the Insights overview, milestones, streaks and active days, goals, your techniques catalog with coach cues, your partners list, and your belt journey timeline.

Sweep Pro opens the deep analysis: per-attempt logging across all five move kinds, the Game, Trends, Partners and Competition insight tabs, positional flow, tournaments, gameplans, working-log notes, partner rivalry breakdowns, the deep belt report, and CSV / JSON export with local backup.
Is it a subscription?
No. Sweep Pro is a single one-time purchase of $12.99. You pay once and it's yours. There is no subscription, ever.
Do I need an account?
No. There is no sign-up, no login, and no email required. You open the app and start logging. Nothing about you is sent anywhere.
Where is my data stored?
On your phone, in the app's local storage. It never touches a server, because Sweep doesn't have one. You can export everything to a JSON or CSV file anytime. See our Privacy Policy for the full picture.
How do I back up or move to a new phone?
Because there's no cloud, your log lives on the device you created it on. With Pro you can export a full JSON backup from Settings and import it on a new phone. Your Pro unlock itself follows your Google account through Google Play, so a quick "restore purchase" brings it back on any device.
Does it work for gi and no-gi?
Both. Tag each session as gi or no-gi when you log it, and you can filter your insights by gi, no-gi, or all. The move library covers both, including leg locks and modern no-gi positions.
Will there be an iPhone version?
Sweep is built so an iPhone version is possible, and Android comes first. There's no date yet. If you want it on iPhone, write in and tell us. It helps us prioritize.
How do I get a refund?
Purchases go through Google Play, so refunds follow Google's process: open the Play Store, go to your order history, and request a refund within Google's window. If you hit any trouble, email us and we'll help.
Is Sweep for kids?
Sweep is built for teen and adult grapplers, ages 13 and up. It's a training log and analysis tool rather than a kids' app.
Do I need to be online to use it?
No. Once installed, Sweep runs fully offline. The only time it needs a connection is the one-time Pro purchase, which Google Play handles.
